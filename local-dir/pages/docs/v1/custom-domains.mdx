<H1>Set up a Custom Domain</H1>

To set up a custom domain for your project, please follow these steps:

### Configure custom domain in project settings

1. Go to the hosting tab in the project settings.
2. Add your custom domain and click save.

<Alert>
  <InfoIcon className="h-4 w-4" />
  <AlertTitle>Note</AlertTitle>

  <AlertDescription>
    You need a paid plan to use this feature. If you haven't upgraded your project plan yet, please do so to add a custom domain.
  </AlertDescription>
</Alert>

### Configure CNAME in your DNS provider

1. Access your DNS provider's settings.
2. Create a CNAME record for your domain directing to `cname.veryfront.com`.
3. Keep in mind that DNS propagation might take a few minutes to finalize.

After completing these steps, you should be able to access your production environment using your newly set up custom domain.

Please note that the steps for configuring your DNS provider may vary depending on the specific hosting provider and DNS management interface you are using. If you encounter any difficulties during the setup process, we recommend referring to the documentation provided by your hosting provider or reaching out to their support team for further assistance. They will be able to provide you with the most accurate and up-to-date instructions based on their platform.
