<H1>Troubleshooting</H1>

If you encounter any issues with your project, you can try the following troubleshooting steps:

1. **Enable Debug Mode**: In the preview context menu (accessible through the three dots), enable the debug mode. This can provide additional information that may help identify the problem.

2. **Check Server and Browser Logs**: Access the logs panel located below the preview area. Review the server and browser logs for any error messages or warnings that could give insights into the issue.

3. **Use Browser Developer Tools**: Utilize browser developer tools, such as Chrome DevTools, to inspect the project and look for any errors, console messages, or network requests that may provide relevant details about the problem.

4. **Contact Support**: If you have a Professional or Enterprise plan, and the issue persists after attempting the previous steps, please reach out to our support team for further assistance. They will be able to provide you with dedicated support and help resolve any complex issues you may be facing.
