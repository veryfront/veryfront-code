<H1>Disable Veryfront Branding</H1>

To disable Veryfront branding for your project, you need to upgrade your plan to either the Professional or Enterprise plan.

To upgrade your plan, please follow these steps:

1. Go to the billing tab in the project settings.
2. Ensure that you are currently on a Free or lower-tier plan. If you haven't upgraded your project plan yet, please do so to disable Veryfront branding for your project.

After completing these steps, the Veryfront badge will no longer be displayed in your project.
