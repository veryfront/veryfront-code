<H1>Team Collaboration</H1>

Collaboration is a key aspect of any project's success. Here is how you can invite your team members to contribute:

1. Initiate by navigating to the project settings. This can be done by locating the gear icon in the sidebar and clicking on it.
2. Once in the settings, direct yourself towards the user management area of your project. Simply select "Users" from the given menu to proceed.
3. Next, input the email address of the team member you wish to invite in the provided field and click the "Invite" button to send them an invitation.
4. An email invitation will be dispatched to your team member. Once they sign up and accept the invitation, they'll have immediate access to the project and can start contributing.
