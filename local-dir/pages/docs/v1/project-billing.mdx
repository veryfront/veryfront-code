<H1>Project billing</H1>

### Setting up billing

1. Go to the project settings by clicking the gear icon in the sidebar.
2. Navigate to the billing area of your project by selecting `Billing` from the menu.
3. Click the `Upgrade Plan` button.
4. Choose a plan e.g. `Standard` and click the `Choose Plan` button.
5. You will be redirected to checkout page. Enter your payment details and confirm by clicking `Subscribe`.
6. You will be brought back to the billing area and should see your active plan details reflected.

### Updating your payment details

1. Go to the project settings by clicking the gear icon in the sidebar.
2. Navigate to the billing area of your project by selecting `Billing` from the menu.
3. Click the `Manage Subscription` button.
4. Here you can update your payment method and billing information.

### Download invoices

1. Go to the project settings by clicking the gear icon in the sidebar.
2. Navigate to the billing area of your project by selecting `Billing` from the menu.
3. Click the `Manage Subscription` button.
4. Here you can find a list of the invoice history which you can download.

### Canceling your plan

1. Go to the project settings by clicking the gear icon in the sidebar.
2. Navigate to the billing area of your project by selecting `Billing` from the menu.
3. Click the `Manage Subscription` button.
4. Here you can cancel your plan by pressing the `Cancel plan` button.
